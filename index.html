Map Creations Through the Ages

Just a small collection of map projects

Florida Waterways

Shows all streams, waterbodies, and other water features present throughout Florida. Urban areas are also used to show relation land use and waterways.

North American Breweries

Hexabin map showing brewery density throughout North America. Inset map dispays the largest density area in greater detail.

The Pacific Crest Trail

Georeferenced Erwin Raisz 'Landforms of the US' map, showing the Pacific Crest Trail path. Details volcanoes, deserts, and waterbodies found along the 2,650 mile trail.

Portland, OR

All the hidden gems of Portland, OR in one map. Anything from breweries, to restaurants, to obscure sites to see.
