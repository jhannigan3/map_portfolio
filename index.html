Map Creations

Small collection of undergraduate and graduate map projects

Trafficking in Europe

Shows human trafficking routes throughout Europe. Includes number of victims per country, as well as GDP.

Created using JavaScript, CSS, Atom Text Editor, and Github.

Portland, OR

All the hidden gems of Portland, OR in one map. Anything from breweries, to restaurants, to obscure sites to see.

Created using JavaScript, CSS, Atom Text Editor, and Github.

Facility Emissions

Facility Emissions by facility type, showing plant names and emissions per ton.

US Power Generation

Depicts power plants, total installed capacity produced by the plant, and respective amounts produced by each fuel source, throghout the US.

Kenya Education

Comparing the national enrollment in primary school, per grade and by county, of boys and girls throughout Kenya in 2014.

Cancer Death Rates

Displays all cancer deaths per 100,000 people, by county, throughout the US. Shows 5-year trend in cancer death rates between 2009-2013.

Correlation Between Human Trafficking, Income, and Prostitution

Demonstrates the correlation between three factors, trafficking, income, and prostitution, in order to show if regulations against prostitution change the habits of trafficking.

Created using ArcGIS.

North American Breweries

Hexabin map showing brewery density throughout North America. Inset map dispays the largest density area in greater detail, with a smaller distance radius.
Created using QGIS.

The Pacific Crest Trail

Georeferenced Erwin Raisz 'Landforms of the US' map, showing the Pacific Crest Trail path. Details volcanoes, deserts, and waterbodies found along the 2,650 mile trail.
Created using QGIS.

Florida Waterways

Shows all streams, waterbodies, and other water features present throughout Florida. Urban areas are also used to show relation land use and waterways.
Created using QGIS.
